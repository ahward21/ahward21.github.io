Game Jam

2023 UT Winter Game Jam

About this project

This was a game jam held at the University of Twente in 2023. I was a solo team, the theme was 'Hearts', and I decided to created a VR wave shooter in Unity with the XR interaction framework.

Project Details

Technologies

Unity 3D, Unity, C#

Duration

48 hours

Role

Lead Developer

Challenges & Solutions

The primary challenge in game jams is balancing creative ambition with technical feasibility within extremely tight deadlines, but with some fun and friends its a great way to learn new things and get creative.

Results

The main goal was to get a gameloop going, I just about managed in the end but there were alot of bugs and half baked ideas. I learned a lot of time saving tips in Unity, and it was a great way to get creative and learn new things.
