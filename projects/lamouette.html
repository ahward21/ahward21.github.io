La Mouette

Vacation rental website

About this project

La Mouette is a vacation rental website created for my family's French vacation house that is rented out during the peak season

Project Details

Tools used

Wordpress, HTML, CSS

Duration

On going

Role

Developer & Designer

Challenges & Solutions

The house was passed down through generations and has a lot of history, so it was important to preserve that while still making it feel modern and inviting. The goal was to advertise the website as family rental to Dutch and English tourists, through the use of wordpress and third party plugins a cheap, functional website was easily achievable.
